About Eskah Edge Limited

Eskah Edge Limited is a reliable provider of cutting-edge technology, targeted marketing, and business solutions focused on achieving growth and impact.

We specialize in:

  • Custom website design (Ecommerce, blogs, web apps), domain registration, web hosting, email hosting, graphics design, website support, and maintenance.
  • Digital marketing, corporate branding, social media management, brand development, influencer marketing, content creation, SEO optimization, and email marketing.
  • Administrative services, including business registrations, statutory returns, and startup & ongoing business support.

Founded in 2023 with a passion for empowering businesses, Eskah Edge leverages a strong foundation in Business Administration and Computer Science to deliver timely, creative, and reliable solutions. We are committed to helping businesses grow, streamline operations, and thrive in competitive markets.
